Feeding Choice Simulator

8th grade — you control who eats whom in the food web

How to play

Each season you get 6 feeding actions. You decide which animals eat which food sources and how much.

  1. Assign feeding — pick predator → prey → amount (or use quick buttons / click arrows on the food web).
  2. End season — the simulation runs: energy transfer (~10% rule), hunger, births, deaths.
  3. Species you forget to feed lose large chunks of population each season.
  4. If you ignore feeding, at least one species will likely go extinct within 4 seasons.
  5. Up to 6 random seasons (out of 12) trigger an environmental event each game.

Valid feeding links

  • 🐛 Aphid / Caterpillar / 🐭 Mouse → 🌿 Grass or ☘️ Clover
  • 🐰 Rabbit → 🌿 Grass or ☘️ Clover
  • 🐦 Blue Tit → 🐛 Aphid or Caterpillar
  • 🦊 Fox → 🐰 Rabbit, 🐦 Blue Tit, or 🐭 Mouse
  • 🦅 Hawk → 🐭 Mouse, 🐰 Rabbit, or 🐦 Blue Tit
  • 🌿 Plants use sunlight · 🍄 Fungi grow when other species die (dead matter)

💡 Strategy: Over-harvest plants and insects collapse the web. Under-feed the fox and rabbits explode — then plants suffer. Balance matters!
